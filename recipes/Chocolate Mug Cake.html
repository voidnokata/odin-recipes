This microwave Chocolate Mug Cake recipe will become your new obsession


Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour and granulated sugar
  • Cocoa Powder, Baking Powder, and Salt
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Milk
  • Canola Oil or Melted Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Chocolate Chips

Directions

  1. Combine Dry Ingredients: Pour flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt in your mug.
  2. Add Wet Ingredients: Pour in milk, canola oil, and vanilla extract. Stir until smooth.
  3. Stir in Chocolate Chips.
  4. Microwave: Cook in the microwave then let this easy chocolate mug cake rest for one minute before diving in!
